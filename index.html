02. My Projects

True of False Quiz:

“True or False” is an entertaining and straight forward trivia game. Players are presented with a statement and have just 15 seconds to decide whether it's true or false. The game covers various categories, from history and science to pop culture and current events. The gameplay is addictive, as players must answer as many questions as possible before time runs out.


CSV & Excel Parser with Team Allocation System:

This application is a web-based team allocation system that allows users to upload CSV or Excel files and allocate team members based on specific criteria. It is coded in HTML, CSS, JS, jQuery, and Papaparse. The system parses CSV and Excel documents, manipulates data, and allocates team members using a custom algorithm. This project aims to showcases my proficiency in web development, JavaScript, and data manipulation.

Login & Registration Site:

I designed and developed a meditation website with registration and login functionality, using a combination of HTML, CSS, PHP, MySQL, and JavaScript. Users can register with their personal details and create a secure account, which allows them to access a variety of guided meditation resources, customized to their preferences and experience level.
